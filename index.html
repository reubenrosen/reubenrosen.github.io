My Projects

Most of my research so far has been in the domain of Artificial Intelligence specialized in healthcare. I also have some projects based on Internet of Things. I know a bit of app development here and there as I've been learning some Flutter and had some prior experience with Java. Every day I'm always learning something new, so the possibilities are endless! Some of my Projects are listed below:

Infant Mood Prediction using Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients

Conducted research in classifying Audio Signals into possible categories of the reason for an infant crying. Different audio signals were trained and classified on the basis of parameters such as Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficient (MFCC). You can view my publication on IEEE Xplore here

COPD Detection in Lungs

Audio Signals of the lungs are analysed using k means clustering and Vector Quantization to classify the audio into a healthy lung or a lung of a COPD patient.

Disease Identification in Eye Fundus using YOLOv5

Research was conducted in the possibility of using YOLOv5 to scan and identify from a possibility of four different diseases in an eye fundus dataset.

COVID-19 Automated Hand Sanitizer with Integrated Face Mask Detector

A webcam connected to a computer scanned the face for the presence of a face mask. A motor controlled a hand sanitizer dispenser with an infrared sensor to disperse the liquid.

Automated Irrigation System with Mobile App

An irrigation system was built which would disperse water depending on the soil moisture, humidity, and temparature considered as factors. Plant and soil parameters could be viewed on the mobile app. The water pump could also be controlled externally via the app.
